# My Journey

A quick look at my college journey and the technical transitions along the way. Stay tuned for more!

## 2025: Stepping into the ML World

- **August:** Cleared the **Amazon ML Summer School 2025** program, diving deep into Machine Learning, NLP, GenAI, and Large Language Models.
- **July - September:** Joined **Soundverse AI** as an Applied AI Intern, working remotely in New York. I got heavily involved in building an Agentic AI assistant, and exposing their music generation APIs to clients like Claude Desktop.
- **May:** Built **VocaAI**, marking my big jump into AI and web apps – successfully creating an AI receptionist handling 24/7 business automation.

## 2024: Web Dev & AI Projects
